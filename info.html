Diego Babino (Ushinaya)

Born on December 31, 2004, in Geneva, Switzerland, I am a photographer working between documentary and conceptual practices. My work explores the infrastructures, technologies, and landscapes that shape contemporary life, with a particular interest in aviation and the networks that connect distant places. Drawn to spaces marked by movement, industry, and absence, I photograph environments where global systems become tangible. Through long-term projects, I seek to reveal the traces, stories, and quiet tensions embedded within the landscapes we often pass through without noticing.
